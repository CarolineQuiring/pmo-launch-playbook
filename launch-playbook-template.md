# Launch Playbook Template

A reusable framework for planning and executing high-visibility product launches across cross-functional teams.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (8-12 Weeks Out)

### Program Setup
- [ ] Define launch goals, KPIs, and success metrics
- [ ] Identify workstream leads and assign owners
- [ ] Build RACI matrix across all teams
- [ ] Establish weekly sync cadence and communication channels
- [ ] Set up project tracker and central source of truth

### Stakeholder Alignment
- [ ] Schedule executive kickoff
- [ ] Align on launch tier (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3)
- [ ] Confirm budget and resource allocation
- [ ] Document decision rights and escalation paths

---

## Phase 2: Build (4-8 Weeks Out)

### Workstream Execution
- [ ] Confirm deliverables and deadlines per workstream
- [ ] Run weekly cross-functional syncs
- [ ] Track dependencies and surface blockers early
- [ ] Review and approve messaging and positioning
- [ ] Confirm event or activation logistics if applicable

### Risk Management
- [ ] Maintain live risk register
- [ ] Flag risks to leadership weekly
- [ ] Document contingency plans for top 3 risks

---

## Phase 3: Launch Week

### Final Checks
- [ ] Launch readiness review with all workstream leads
- [ ] Confirm all assets approved and ready
- [ ] Brief executive stakeholders
- [ ] Activate internal communications
- [ ] Go live

### Launch Day
- [ ] Monitor all channels in real time
- [ ] Track engagement and performance metrics
- [ ] Escalate issues immediately with clear owner and resolution

---

## Phase 4: Post-Launch

### Review and Optimize
- [ ] Conduct post-launch retrospective within 2 weeks
- [ ] Document what worked and what did not
- [ ] Capture metrics against KPIs
- [ ] Update playbook based on learnings
- [ ] Share results with executive stakeholders

---

## Tools Used
- Smartsheet for project tracking
- Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace for documentation and reporting
- Slack for real-time communication
- AI tools (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT) for workflow automation and documentation
