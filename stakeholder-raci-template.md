# Stakeholder RACI Template

A framework for defining roles and responsibilities across cross-functional program teams.

---

## How to Use This Template

- **R = Responsible** — Does the work
- **A = Accountable** — Owns the outcome, final decision maker
- **C = Consulted** — Provides input before decisions are made
- **I = Informed** — Kept up to date on progress and decisions

---

## Launch Program RACI

| Workstream | Program Manager | Executive Sponsor | Workstream Lead | Cross-Functional Partner | External Agency |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Launch Strategy | A | A | R | C | I |
| Messaging and Positioning | C | A | R | C | I |
| Event / Activation Planning | A | I | R | C | R |
| Budget Management | A | A | R | I | C |
| Executive Communications | R | A | C | I | I |
| Vendor Management | A | I | C | I | R |
| Risk Management | A | C | R | C | I |
| Post-Launch Review | R | I | C | C | I |

---

## Decision Rights

- **Executive Sponsor** — Final approval on budget, strategy, and go/no-go decisions
- **Program Manager** — Day-to-day decisions, escalation point for workstream leads
- **Workstream Lead** — Owns execution decisions within their workstream
- **Cross-Functional Partner** — Input on decisions that impact their team

---

## Escalation Path

1. Workstream Lead attempts to resolve
2. Program Manager steps in if unresolved within 24 hours
3. Executive Sponsor engaged for decisions requiring budget or strategy change
